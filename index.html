Why audit a smart contract?

An external audit of your smart contract will help to identify errors in the code, vulnerabilities, and check the program logic. A professional and high-quality audit will save your nerves and money.

Is smart contract audit expensive?

Smart contract auditing is one of the most important tasks in blockchain projects, as security should be a priority to avoid important issues once the code is run.

Failure to do so could result in lost and lost thousands of dollars, or, at best, more gas wasted in contracts than necessary due to inefficiency.

Keep in mind that when you manage smart contracts, you usually deal with a lot of money from people who have trusted you. Imagine the problems you would face if, after a mass sale, if it gets hacked and all the money is lost.
